Gochujang Chicken Rice Bowl

Home

Description

A Gochujang Chicken Rice Bowl typically features rice tipped with seasoned chicken cooked in a flavorful Gochujang-based sauce, often accompanied by vegetables and a garnish like sesame seeds.


Ingredients:

  • Chicken: 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bit-sized pieces.
  • Rice: @ cups cooked rice (jasmine, sushi, or your preference).
  • Gochujang Sauce:
    • 4 tbsp Gochujang (Korean chili paste).
    • 2 tbsp soy sauce.
    • 2 tbsp honey
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced.
    • 1 tbsp grated ginger.
    • 1 tbsp sesame oil.
    • 1 tbsp rice vinegar (optional).

  • Vegetables:
    • 1 cup sliced bell peppers (various colors).
    • 1/2 cup sliced onion.
    • 1 cup shredded carrots.
    • 1 cup frozen peas or edamame.

  • Garnish:
    • 2 tbsp sesame seeds, toasted.
    • 2 green onions, sliced.

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, combine the chicken, Gochujang, soy sauce, honey, garlic, ginger, sesame oil, and rice vinegar (if using). Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or longer for deeper flavor.
  2. Cook the rice: Prepare your rice according to package instructions.
  3. Cook the chicken: Heat a skillet or work over medium-high heat with a little oil. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir-fry vegetables: In the same skillet, add a little more oil if needed, then add the sliced bell peppers, onion, and carrots. Stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  5. Assemble the bowls: Divide the cooked rice into bowls. Top with the Gochujang chicken and stir-fried vegetables.
  6. Garnish and serve:Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds and sliced green onions. Serve immediately.
