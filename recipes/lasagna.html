Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.


Ingredients:

  • Noodles: Lasagna noodles (oven-ready or regular, boiled until al dente.
  • Meat Sauce: Ground meat (beef, pork or a combination), onion garlic, canned crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  • Cheese Mixture: Ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper
  • Other Cheeses: Mozzarella (for layering and topping).
  • Optional additions:Provolone or other cheeses, fresh herbs like basil or parsley, spinach.
